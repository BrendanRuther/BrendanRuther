Escape To Mars

The year is 2100. Humanity is at the brink of extinction. Climate change has accelerated and Earth is nearly uninhabitable. The last hope for survival is starting a colony on Mars. You are the lead flight director on the world's only space program. Scientists say we have 22 years before the entire population dies out. Since Earth aligns with Mars every 26 months you will have 10 windows for launch. Your score is based on which missions you complete. Successfully complete all missions to save the world! Use arrow keys or WASD to move. Remember you have limited fuel when landing.

Pirate Invasion

Pirate Invasion is a fun python game I created. The more ships you hit the faster they move. Don;t let them reach your ship or your treasure will be stolen. The game is loosely based off the arcade classic Galaga.
